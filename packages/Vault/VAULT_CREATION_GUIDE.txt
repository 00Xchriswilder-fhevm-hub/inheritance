                    HOW TO CREATE A LEGACYVAULT
                    Simple Step-by-Step Guide
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

STEP 1: CONNECT YOUR WALLET
───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
• Click "Connect Wallet" button
• Select your wallet (MetaMask, WalletConnect, etc.)
• Approve the connection
• Make sure you're on Sepolia Testnet (required for FHEVM)

STEP 2: CHOOSE CONTENT TYPE
───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
• Select "Secret Text" for recovery phrases, mnemonics, or text
• OR select "File" to upload documents, images, or any file

STEP 3: ENTER YOUR CONTENT
───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
• If Text: Type or paste your secret (like a 12-word recovery phrase)
• If File: Click to upload your file (max 5MB)

STEP 4: ADD HEIRS
───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
• Enter Ethereum wallet addresses of people who should access the vault
• You can add multiple heirs
• They will only be able to unlock after the release date

STEP 5: SET RELEASE DATE
───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
• Choose a date and time when the vault should unlock
• This is when your heirs can access the content
• You (the owner) can unlock anytime before this date

STEP 6: REVIEW & CREATE
───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
• Review all your information
• Click "Create Vault"
• Approve the transaction in your wallet
• Wait for confirmation (this encrypts and stores your vault)

STEP 7: GRANT ACCESS (OPTIONAL)
───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
• If you added heirs, approve the "Grant Access" transaction
• This gives them permission to unlock after the release date

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
                              THAT'S IT!
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

Your vault is now:
✓ Encrypted with AES-256-GCM
✓ Stored on IPFS (decentralized storage)
✓ Keys encrypted with FHE and stored on blockchain
✓ Time-locked until your chosen release date
✓ Accessible by you anytime, or by heirs after release date

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
                           IMPORTANT NOTES
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

• Keep your vault ID safe (you'll need it to unlock)
• Only you can unlock before the release date
• Heirs can only unlock after the release date
• All data is encrypted - even we can't see it
• Works on Sepolia Testnet only
